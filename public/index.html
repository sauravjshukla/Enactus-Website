ENACTUS BITS GOA







Mission Statement

Enactus, formely known as Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE) is an international non-profit organisation of students present in over 1600 universities spread across 39 Countries and has over 67,000 students as active members. Enactus students take up community outreach projects to transform lives of people in need and shape a better world, using the power of entrepreneurial actions.

Enactus is an entrepreneurial body of students and future leaders to provide for the innovate solutions for world problems through social help and change.

At Enactus, we collaborate with several NGOs and self-help groups, putting our boots on the ground to make a tangible change for a positive impact on society and nature. Viewing incompetence with indifference makes us all failing citizens of this world, and hence Enactus BITS Goa strives to restore the social and ecological balance that has existed for long, but is threatened in the recent times. At Enactus, we believe that every individual can make an impact in their community - not in the form of charity - but by using the positive power of entrepreneurship. It's a community of not just students but also academic and business leaders- in partnership with corporations and organizations - committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better more sustainable world. Our student body perseveres to achieve fixed outcomes, just like a start-up, however, with a non-profit, goals-oriented approach, we are much better than any capitalist organization. We are just a couple of students with big hearts, and a will to work, and we are optimistic that our efforts will not go in vain.
